Game Rules

This is a game that's inspired from The Simon Game, it is a classic memory game that challenges players to remember a series of colors and sounds in sequence. Here are the basic rules:

  1. You will be given a sequence of colors and sounds in a specific order.
  2. Your goal is to repeat the sequence exactly as you were given.
  3. If you succeed, another color and sound will be added to the sequence.
  4. You must continue to repeat the sequence, which will grow longer and more challenging.
  5. If you make a mistake and repeat the sequence incorrectly, the game ends.
  6. The game will keep track of your high score until you refresh the page, so you can try to beat your best performance.

Enjoy the game and test your memory skills!
